The Kaggle Experience from a Digital Analyst's Perspective

1 February 2019 Kaggle

Digital Analytics Thessaloniki is one of the most popular and active tech meetups in the city. I had the opportunity to share my experience of taking part in the Kaggle competition with Google Analytics data.

The presentation focused not on the competition itself, but rather on the advantages digital analysts can gain from leveraging Kaggle. Kaggle now encompasses much more than just competitions.

Presentation: The Kaggle Experience from a Digital Analysts' Perspective by Alexandros Papageorgiou.

Version 2.0 of this talk was presented at MeasureCamp London, and version 3.0 was presented at MeasureCamp Dublin.
